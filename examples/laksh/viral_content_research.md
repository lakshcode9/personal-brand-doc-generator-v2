## Key Patterns

### What Makes Content Go Viral

1. **Fear + Solution hooks** — "AI will replace you... unless you do X" crushes on YouTube
2. **Speed demos** — 60-second AI builds get insane virality on Instagram
3. **Personal story format** — Justin Welsh dominates LinkedIn with narrative + universal lesson
4. **Contrarian takes** — "Why 95% fail" or "Stop doing X, do Y" drives comments
5. **Tool roundups** — Reliable YouTube format. "Best 10 AI tools" = consistent views
6. **Origin stories** — Your dropout-to-architect narrative is underused and highly engaging

### Platform Cheat Sheet

| Platform | Best Format | Optimal Length | How to Win |
| ---------- | ------------- | ---------------- | ------------ |
| YouTube | Tutorials, tool demos, "how I did it" | 8-15 min long / < 60s shorts | Views ÷ Subs ≥ 3x |
| Instagram | Speed demos, AI hacks, aspirational POVs | 15-45 seconds | Views ÷ Followers ≥ 3x |
| LinkedIn | Personal stories, hot takes, credibility posts | 150-300 words | Raw engagement total (L+C+S) |
