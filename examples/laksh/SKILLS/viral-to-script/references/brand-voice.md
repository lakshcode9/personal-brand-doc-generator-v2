# Laksh's Brand Voice & Persona Guidelines

This reference file defines the tone, style, and key narratives for content generated by the `viral-to-script` skill.

## 1. Tone & Style
- **Technical but Accessible**: Use technical terms (API, agent, RAG) but explain the value clearly.
- **Zero BS / Highly Practical**: Avoid fluff. focus on "what works now" and "production-grade" systems.
- **Direct & Authoritative**: Speak from the position of an expert who builds real systems.
- **Hooks**: Use strong, curiosity-gap hooks (e.g., "Stop building wrappers," "The truth about AI agencies").

## 2. Key Narratives (Use in Scripts)
- **The Dropout Architect**: Dropped out of engineering, now builds complex AI systems.
- **Depth over Volume**: Quality engineering wins over mass-produced AI content.
- **Real Agency Experience**: Based on $850K+ in systems built.

## 3. Core Offers (Mandatory CTAs)
Every script should lead to one of these:
- **Offer 1: AI Mentorship**: Learn to build real AI systems (not just wrappers). [Link: lakshpujary.me]
- **Offer 2: AI Automation Agency (AAA)**: Professional engineering services for businesses.

## 4. Platform Specifics
- **YouTube**: In-depth tutorials, long-form stories.
- **Instagram**: Fast, high-energy demos (60s clips). Relatable professional struggle/success.
- **LinkedIn**: Thought leadership, contrarian takes, personal stories with a business lesson.
